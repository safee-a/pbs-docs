# Timeline and Cycles

The PBS listing process follows a predictable annual cycle driven by PBAC meeting dates. Understanding these timelines helps predict when new listings will appear in the API.

## PBAC Meeting Schedule

PBAC meets **three times per year**. Each meeting evaluates submissions lodged by the corresponding deadline.

| Meeting Month | Approximate Submission Deadline | Results Published |
|---------------|-------------------------------|-------------------|
| March | November (prior year) | April-May |
| July | March | August-September |
| November | July | December-January |

<Aside type="note">
Submission deadlines are approximately **4 months** before the meeting. Exact dates vary each year and are published on the PBAC website.
</Aside>

## Typical Timeline

The end-to-end process from submission to PBS listing has several stages:

| Stage | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| PBAC submission to meeting | 4 months |
| PBAC evaluation and decision | 1-2 months (at meeting) |
| Post-meeting outcome publication | 1-2 months |
| PBPA price negotiation | 1-4 months |
| Minister approval | 1-2 months |
| Next available schedule update | 1-2 months |
| **Total (first attempt)** | **6-12 months** |

### If Deferred or Rejected

- **Deferred** — PBAC requests more information. The sponsor provides additional data and the submission is reconsidered at the next meeting. This adds **4-8 months**.
- **Rejected** — the sponsor must prepare a new submission with additional evidence. Resubmission adds **4-12 months** depending on evidence requirements.
- **Total with resubmission** — can extend to **17 months or longer**.

## Monthly Schedule Updates

Regardless of when PBAC makes its recommendation, new listings only appear in the PBS on the **1st of each month** when the schedule is updated.

<Aside type="tip">
**Agent tip:** To detect new listings, compare consecutive schedules using the [`summary-of-changes`](/endpoints/summary-of-changes/) endpoint. Each schedule is identified by its `schedule_code` in `YYYY-MM-01` format.
</Aside>

## Predicting New Listings

After a positive PBAC recommendation, new listings typically appear **2-4 months later** once pricing is agreed. You can monitor upcoming changes by:

1. Checking PBAC outcome publications on [pbac.pbs.gov.au](https://pbac.pbs.gov.au)
2. Watching the [`summary-of-changes`](/endpoints/summary-of-changes/) endpoint each month
3. Comparing [`items`](/endpoints/items/) data between consecutive `schedule_code` values

## Annual Pattern

The annual cycle creates a predictable rhythm:

- **January-February** — November PBAC outcomes finalized, new listings start appearing
- **March** — first PBAC meeting of the year
- **April-June** — March PBAC outcomes published, price negotiations active
- **July** — second PBAC meeting
- **August-October** — July PBAC outcomes published, new listings appear
- **November** — third PBAC meeting
- **December** — November outcomes begin publication
